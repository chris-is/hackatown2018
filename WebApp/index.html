WatchMTL

How it works

Say something? Whether is a crime, a fire, a broken street lamp, help the city improve its infrastructure and service by reporting anonymously at watchmtl.com.

Smart city

WatchMTL is a smart app powered by Google Cloud Platform ,using machine learning on computer vision and natural language processing. WatchMTL aims to provide a way for residents to build a smart city.

Fast & Anonymously

WatchMTL is fast and provides user with anonymity. Residents can report something without worrying about personal information being leaked.
