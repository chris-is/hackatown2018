watchMTL

How it works

Do you see something that needs to be reported? Whether it's a crime, an accident, a broken street lamp, or a natural disaster, help your city take the necessary actions by reporting the incident anonymously at watchMTL.com.

Smart city

watchMTL is a smart app powered by the Google Cloud Platform, using machine learning on computer vision and natural language processing. watchMTL aims to provide a way for residents to build a smarter, and safer city.

Fast & Anonymously

watchMTL is fast and provides users with anonymity. Residents can report incidents without worrying about personal information being leaked.
